Structural Strengthening Services

We specialize in Carbon Fibre Reinforced Polymer (CFRP) systems—a lightweight, durable, and cost-effective method for strengthening a wide range of structures. CFRP based solutions consist of CFRP plates and rods, plus structural epoxy resin-based adhesives.

Applications

Our CFRP technology extends the life of any structure with minimal disruption, including:

  • Residential walls, beams, and roofs
  • Bridges and culverts
  • Industrial buildings
  • Structural elements requiring increased load capacity

Key Advantages of CFRP Structural Strengthening Systems

Quick Installation

Easy and efficient to apply, with no additional plate preparation needed. A single product is used for surface filling, priming, and bonding—reducing downtime and speeding up project delivery.

Rapid Curing

Fast-setting materials mean minimal disruption to site operations and faster return to service.

Exceptional Durability

Rigorously tested under demanding conditions to guarantee long-term performance in a wide range of environments and applications.

Seamless Appearance

Designed for both external and near-surface mounted (NSM) applications, ensuring there's no visual impact on the existing structure.

Cost-Effective

Combines long service life with reduced installation time, offering a highly efficient and economical strengthening solution.
